Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

Effective Date: June 12, 2026

1. Local Data

Your App data is stored locally on your device.

We do not use your personal profiles, scripts, stories, image selections, or API configurations.

2. Local Storage

Locally stored data may include personal profiles, scripts and stories, image selections, API endpoints, model names, and API keys.

On mobile devices, API keys are stored using the system's secure storage mechanisms. In the web preview environment, API keys are only stored during the current browser session.

3. Device Permissions

Photo Library or Media Library permissions are used solely to allow you to select avatars, wallpapers, card backgrounds, widget photos, or script cover images.

File access permissions are used solely to allow you to select script files and read their contents on your device.

These permissions are only used to perform actions that you explicitly initiate within the App.

4. Third-Party APIs

When you choose to use third-party API features, related content may be transmitted from your device to the API service that you configure.

The handling of such data is governed by the terms and privacy policies of the respective third-party service.

5. Your Control

You may view or modify your profiles, script settings, and story content within the App.

You may delete local scripts, story copies, or remove API configurations at any time.

You may also clear App data through your device settings. Once deleted or uninstalled, MoniMoni cannot recover any locally stored data.

6. Protection of Minors

MoniMoni is not intended to provide services directly to children under the age of 14.

Minors should use the service only with the consent and guidance of a parent or legal guardian.

7. Updates and Contact

The latest version of this Privacy Policy will be made available within the App.

If you have any questions, suggestions, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the "Settings → Feedback & Wishes" section within the App.
